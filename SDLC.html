SDLC IS A PHASED APPROACH TO ANALYSIS & DESIGN OF INFORMATION SYSTEM

SDLC
IDENTIFYING PROBLEMS:OPPORTUNITIES & OBJECTIVES
DETERMINING:INFORMATION REQUIREMENTS

SDLC is a set of activities carried out by the system analysts & system designers to develop an information system.SDLC consists of identifying problems,opportunities and objectives,determining information requirements,analysing system needs,designing the recommending systems,developing and documenting software,testing and maintaining the system and ultimately implementing as well as evaluating the system.
